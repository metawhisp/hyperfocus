What it does

It doesn't just count down. It stages your entry into work.

Every part is designed to get you in — and keep you there.

Focus Orb

A small always-on-top glass dot floating on your screen. Drag it anywhere, click to start. That's the whole interface.

Cinematic countdown

The screen darkens, 3 · 2 · 1 · FOCUS counts you in with voice. A real threshold between scrolling and working.

Edge aura

A soft green glow lines your screen while you focus. Drift away and it turns red — a calm, wordless signal you're off track.

Presence detection

Your camera checks you're still there — processed entirely on-device. Leave your desk and the timer pauses itself.

Away mode

Gone too long? The aura goes red, a soft alarm calls you back, and focus resumes the moment you return.

Stats & 100 achievements

Every session is logged locally — streaks, laser-focus rate, best hours — with 100 unique badges to unlock.

How it works

Three seconds from distracted to locked in.

Click the orb

Tap the floating dot. A single glass card asks what you're working on.

Name one task

Type your mission, pick a duration and intensity. One task — not a list.

Enter the countdown

The screen dims, the count begins, and you're in. Nothing else to decide.

The camera check never leaves your Mac. Presence detection runs locally with Apple's Vision framework. No video is recorded, uploaded, or sent anywhere — the camera is only used to notice when you walk away.
